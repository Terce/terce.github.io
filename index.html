Online Prescriptions

Audience:

  • Doctors that need to prescript a drug to their patients
  • Patients who have a drug prescription.
  • Pharmacists who need a prescription in order to sell the patient a drug.

Functionality:

Our web page has two parts:

The first part consists of a login page, here you are presented with several options at the top where you can access other web pages, in the middle you will find an image and two forms that need to be filled in order to have access to your prescription. The first form will ask your full name, once this is complete you will have to type your password.

Once this process is complete you will be able to access your prescription, on the left you will find your diagnose and the drugs that you can use to treat your condition. On the right you will find the date of the prescription, your name, the name of your doctor and your medical history.

To use the prescription you will need to go to a drug store where the pharmacist will be able to check your prescription, give you the drugs you need and mark the prescription as used.

On the top right corner you will have a button to log-out of your account, in your medical history you will be able to see the date and the prescriptions you have received during the last year.

Problems to Solve:
